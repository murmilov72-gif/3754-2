About IK Markets

Empowering financial success through expertise, integrity, and innovation since 2010

Who We Are

IK Markets is a premier financial information consulting firm headquartered in Leeds, England, with a global footprint spanning Europe, Asia, and North America. Founded in 2010, we began as a small team of financial experts with a shared vision: to make sophisticated financial guidance accessible to individuals and businesses of all sizes.

Today, we serve over 2,000 clients worldwide, ranging from high-net-worth individuals to mid-market enterprises and multinational corporations. Our team of 50+ certified financial consultants, analysts, and strategists brings collective expertise across 15+ financial sectors, ensuring we deliver tailored solutions that align with your unique goals and risk profile.

  • 14+ years of industry leadership and proven track record
  • ISO 9001 certified financial consulting services
  • Multilingual team supporting 8+ languages for global clients
  • Consistently rated 4.9/5 by clients for service excellence
